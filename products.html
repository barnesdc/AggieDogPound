Our staff made up of Aggie students is trained to provide the best experience for your dog. The staff team will monitor your dog's health and keep records of their stay. Fresh water will be provided consistently to keep your dog hydrated. Owners are allowed to bring food from home in pre-measured and pre-marked baggies or we can provide premium pet food.

From Around the World Personal attention

When boarding with Aggie Dog #, owners can turn in specific information about their dog. Our staff will follow this information to ensure proper care and to replicate their home environment as closely as possible.

Information can include:

  • Feeding patterns and preferences
  • Medical history and medications
  • How your dog interacts with other dogs
  • What makes your dog happy or upset
